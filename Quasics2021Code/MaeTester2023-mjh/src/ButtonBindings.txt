For demo mode (1 Logitech controller, bottom switch in "D" mode):
* Left/Right Joystick: Tank drive
* A button: Run conveyor backward (push ball backward)
* B button: Run conveyor foreward (push ball into shooter)
* Left shoulder: Raise shooter at high position
* Right shoulder: Raise shooter at low position
* X button: Run shooter at full speed
* Y button: Run shooter at low speed
