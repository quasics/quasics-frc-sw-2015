* Setting up a new project: `python3 -m robotpy init`
* After editing `pyproject.toml`: `python3 -m robotpy sync`
* To run simulation: `python3 -m robotpy sim`

Resources:
* Docs: https://robotpy.readthedocs.io/projects/robotpy/en/stable/
* Examples: https://github.com/robotpy/examples/tree/main
  * Arm simulation: https://github.com/robotpy/examples/blob/main/ArmSimulation/subsytems/arm.py
  * Elevator simulation: https://github.com/robotpy/examples/tree/main/ElevatorSimulation
